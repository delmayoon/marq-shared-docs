Pricing Plan — Channel Pricing Units Guide

A quick reference for which channels are priced per Keyword × Platform vs. Brand × Platform.
May 12, 2026 · v3 Pricing Plan (effective Jan 9, fully enforced after Mar 6, 2026)
Why this document? v3 플랜에서는 더 이상 unlimited coverage가 아닌, 채널별 과금 단위(Keyword × Platform, Brand × Platform 등)로 과금합니다. 간혹 마켓플레이스를 Brand × Platform으로 판매하는 사례가 있어, 각 채널의 정확한 과금 단위를 다시 정리합니다.
Why this document? Under v3, we no longer offer unlimited coverage. Instead, channels are priced by specific units (Keyword × Platform, Brand × Platform, etc.). There have been cases where marketplaces were incorrectly sold as Brand × Platform — this guide clarifies the correct pricing unit for each channel.

핵심 구분

Keyword × Platform = Search → 마켓플레이스에서 사용. 키워드 1개 × 플랫폼 1개 = 1 Search
Brand × Platform → 소셜미디어, 웹사이트, Meta Ads 등에서 사용. 브랜드 단위로 과금
Asset × Platform → Piracy 채널 전용. 콘텐츠 타이틀(Asset) 단위로 과금
Model × Platform × Frequency → Brand Intelligence (Price Monitoring) 전용

Core Distinction

Keyword × Platform = Search → Used for marketplaces. 1 keyword × 1 platform = 1 Search
Brand × Platform → Used for social media, websites, Meta Ads. Charged per brand
Asset × Platform → Piracy channels only. Charged per content title (asset)
Model × Platform × Frequency → Brand Intelligence (Price Monitoring) only

* Model = SKU. Charged by number of SKUs monitored, NOT by searches

* Brand Intelligence is NOT about Saturation Rates or Searches — it finds mis-priced listings impacting PPM

* Hourly monitoring is supported at no additional charge for Brand's owned listings
